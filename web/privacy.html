Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025

Introduction

Currency Converter ("we", "our", or "the extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our browser extension.

üîí Your Privacy is Our Priority

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. The extension operates entirely within your browser and does not send any data to external servers beyond what's necessary for currency rate updates.

Information We Access

1. Web Page Content

The extension reads web page content to identify prices and currency symbols. This processing happens entirely within your browser. We do not store or transmit this information.

2. Extension Settings

Your preferences are stored locally in your browser's storage:

  • Selected currencies for conversion
  • Base currency preference
  • Visual styling preferences
  • Disabled website patterns
  • Theme preference (light/dark)

These settings sync across your devices if you're signed into your browser account.

3. Exchange Rate Data

The extension fetches current exchange rates from public APIs:

  • Fiat currencies: Frankfurter API (api.frankfurter.dev)
  • Cryptocurrencies: CoinGecko API (api.coingecko.com)

These requests contain no personal information and are identical for all users. Rates are cached locally to minimize API requests.

Information We Don't Collect

  • Browsing history
  • Personal identification information
  • Website passwords or form data
  • Analytics or usage statistics
  • Location data (beyond timezone for currency detection)

Third-Party Services

The extension uses third-party APIs solely for obtaining exchange rates. These services may have their own privacy policies:

  • Frankfurter API Privacy
  • CoinGecko Privacy Policy

Website Analytics

This privacy policy website uses Google Analytics to help us understand visitor interest and improve our product. The analytics data collected includes:

  • Pages visited and time spent on pages
  • General geographic location (country/city level)
  • Browser and device type
  • How you arrived at our website

This data is aggregated and anonymous. We use it solely to understand which features generate the most interest and how we can better serve our users. You can opt out of analytics tracking by declining cookies when prompted or by using browser extensions that block analytics scripts.

Important: The Currency Converter browser extension itself does not use Google Analytics or any tracking mechanisms. Analytics are only used on this informational website.

Data Security

All data remains within your browser's secure storage. We implement the following security measures:

  • No external data transmission beyond rate APIs
  • HTTPS-only connections for API requests
  • Local storage encryption handled by your browser
  • No user tracking or analytics in the browser extension

Your Rights

You have complete control over the extension:

  • Disable it on specific websites
  • Clear all settings through your browser
  • Uninstall at any time
  • View the source code (if open source)

Children's Privacy

The extension does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13 years of age. The extension has no age restrictions as it collects no personal data.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date. Continued use of the extension after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or the extension's privacy practices, please contact us through:

  • The feedback form on our website
  • Opening an issue on our GitHub repository
  • The browser extension store listing

Compliance

This extension is designed to comply with privacy laws including GDPR, CCPA, and other applicable regulations by not collecting any personal data.
